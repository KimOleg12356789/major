Why do we believe we provide one of the best customer service in the country?

Reason

1

Exceptional Punctuality

We know our customers are busy people and we appreciate that about them. That's why we don't reschedule, we always leave a reserve after each job so that our technician arrives at your exact scheduled time, regardless of traffic or other unpleasantness. By ordering our services, you can safely plan your day.

2

Professionalism And Experience

All our technicians are absolute professionals in their field, so you can't get a job with us just by registering in the application or remotely, as, unfortunately, you can in almost all companies. To start performing orders under our brand, each technician must undergo a mandatory 2-month training and pass exams.

3

Cleanliness Before, During And After The Work

Our company has high standards of order fulfillment, which allows us to guarantee a neat and pleasant appearance of employees in company uniforms. We prepare and after require each technician to keep your home extremely clean during the order and afterward. You don't have to worry about the safety of your floors, dirt or similar items - it doesn't happen with us.

4

Warranty On All Work And Parts

One of the most important aspects of providing a good service is the warranty, so you can relax and enjoy your home appliances working correctly. All of our work is guaranteed from 1 year and we often have a parts warranty of up to 5 years. No one else on the market can boast the same.

5

Fast Spare Parts When You Need Them

We have been in business for a long time, so in addition to our own spare parts warehouse, we have all deliveries set up in the shortest possible time. Our reputation allows us to get any parts much faster than our competitors at the lowest prices, which in turn allows you to get working equipment on the same day at very nice prices.
